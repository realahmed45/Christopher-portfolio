About Me

I'm a Product Design student at UC Davis with a strong foundation in UX/UI, digital content management, and human-centered design. Currently, I work as a Marketing and Communications Specialist at UC Davis' Graduate School of Management, where I manage web content, create digital materials, and build HTML email campaigns while collaborating with cross-functional teams.

Alongside my studies, I serve as an AI Generalist with the MOVE AI Fellowship, evaluating multimodal AI outputs and applying design principles to assess AI-generated branding and UX content. My experience spans visual brand design for nonprofits including Child Survival Aid Ghana and FilAm ARTS, social media design for mental health advocacy with Un/Do Mindset, and professional marketing campaigns for educational institutions.

I blend analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to craft accessible, visually compelling experiences. My approach centers on strategic thinking, accessibility, and user-centered design principles—always striving to create solutions that balance aesthetics with functionality.
