About Me

Product Designer • UC Davis Student • Creative Problem Solver

Hello, I'm Christopher

I'm a Product Design student at UC Davis with a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences that balance aesthetics with functionality. Currently, I work as a Marketing and Communications Specialist at UC Davis' Graduate School of Management and as an AI Generalist with the MOVE AI Fellowship.

What I Do

My work spans digital content management, visual brand design, social media design, and UX/UI. I've collaborated with organizations including UC Davis GSM, Child Survival Aid Ghana, FilAm ARTS, and Un/Do Mindset to deliver impactful design solutions.

At UC Davis GSM, I manage web content updates, create digital materials for recruitment and engagement, and build HTML email campaigns. I design graphics for social, web, and print channels while collaborating with cross-functional teams including Admissions, Alumni, and Corporate Relations.

My Approach

I believe great design emerges from understanding both user needs and business objectives. My process centers on strategic thinking, accessibility, and human-centered design principles. I blend analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to craft experiences that are both visually compelling and functionally excellent.
