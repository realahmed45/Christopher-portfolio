About Me

UI/UX & Product Designer • UC Davis Student • Design Thinker

Hello, I'm Christopher

I'm a UI/UX and Product Design student at the University of California, Davis. I enjoy turning complex ideas into designs people can actually use.

What I Do

I currently work as a Marketing and Communications Specialist at UC Davis' Graduate School of Management. In this role, I manage web content, design digital assets, and build HTML email campaigns with cross-functional teams.

Outside of class, I work on contract with Handshake's MOVE AI Fellowship. I review multimodal AI outputs and apply design principles to evaluate AI-generated branding and UX work. My experience also includes visual branding for nonprofits such as Child Survival Aid Ghana and The Association for the Advancement of Filipino American Arts and Culture. I have designed social media content for mental health advocacy with Un/Do Mindset and contributed to marketing campaigns for educational institutions.

My Approach

I design with structure in mind without letting it limit creativity. Accessibility and clarity guide my decisions. I focus on creating work that feels purposeful, easy to use, and visually straightforward.
