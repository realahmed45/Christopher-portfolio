FilAm Teen Council Logo System

Created a comprehensive three-part logo system including full-color, black-and-white, and animated GIF versions. Developed through multiple rounds of feedback from initial sketches to final delivery.

Client The Association for the Advancement of Filipino American Arts and Culture
Timeline July 10 – August 4, 2025
Role Branding

The Challenge

Design a logo system that represents Filipino American youth while being versatile enough for various applications, including animation for digital platforms.

The Solution

I began with initial sketches and color concepts, refined the design through collaborative feedback sessions, and delivered a complete three-part system. The animated GIF version adds dynamic appeal for social media and digital presentations.
