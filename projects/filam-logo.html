FilAm Teen Council Logo System

Developed a comprehensive three-part logo system inspired by Filipino cultural symbols, featuring full-color, black-and-white, and animated versions for flexible brand deployment.

Client The Association for the Advancement of Filipino American Arts and Culture
Timeline July 10–Aug 4, Sept 28, 2025
Role Branding

The Challenge

FilAm ARTS needed a versatile logo system for their Teen Council that celebrated Filipino cultural heritage while appealing to youth audiences. The identity had to work across digital platforms, print materials, and video content, requiring three distinct deliverables with consistent visual language.

The Solution

I researched Filipino tattoo patterns, focusing on binunga motifs and traveler patterns. I incorporated subtle geometric elements into a circular frame. The central icon is inspired by a sun with an iris-like center and symbolizes creativity, vision, and cultural heritage. The full-color version uses warm, rustic earth tones to reflect Filipino culture. The black-and-white version features stronger typography for improved legibility at small sizes. The animated GIF adds energy for social media and video intros.
