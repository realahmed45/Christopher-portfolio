Website Banner Redesign

Redesigned website hero banner for Child Survival Aid Ghana, preserving their symbolic imagery of two children on an open book while creating a modern, high-resolution design aligned with their evolving brand.

Client Child Survival Aid Ghana
Timeline May 31–June 1, Aug 4–6, 2024
Role Web Design

The Challenge

CSAG's existing banner lacked visual clarity and their logo resolution was insufficient for modern web displays. The original concept of two children on an open book needed to be preserved while improving overall design quality and aligning with their upcoming website launch.

The Solution

Working with Emmanuel Adika, I reviewed existing graphics and translated them into a clean, modern banner concept. I rebuilt the banner from scratch at high resolution with a color palette derived from the updated logo, prioritizing visual harmony with the new website's green and blue tones. Through feedback rounds, I adjusted color intensity, refined edges, optimized contrast, and created both standard and text-free versions.

✓

✓
