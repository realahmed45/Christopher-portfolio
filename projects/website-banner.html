Website Banner Redesign

Redesigned CSAG's website banner and created a high-resolution version of their logo. Recreated their original concept featuring two children on an open book with refined spacing and clarity.

Client Child Survival Aid Ghana
Timeline May 31 – August 6, 2024
Role Web Design

The Challenge

The existing website banner lacked visual clarity and the logo resolution was insufficient for modern web displays. The original concept of two children on an open book needed to be preserved while improving overall design quality.

The Solution

I carefully recreated the two children on an open book concept, focusing on cleaner lines, improved spacing and better visual hierarchy. The high-resolution logo recreation ensures crisp display across all devices and screen sizes.
