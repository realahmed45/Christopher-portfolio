Logo Redesign

Modernized Child Survival Aid Ghana's organizational logo by digitally rebuilding it from scratch as clean vector artwork with enhanced colors, multiple variations, and improved scalability.

Client Child Survival Aid Ghana
Timeline May 31–June 1, Aug 4–6, 2024
Role Branding

The Challenge

CSAG's original logo concept with two children standing on an open book was meaningful. However, it had low resolution, inconsistent colors, and outdated styling. The organization needed a professional identity that preserved the core symbolism and met modern standards for digital and print use.

The Solution

Working with Director Emmanuel Adika, I digitally rebuilt the entire logo from scratch in high resolution, creating clean vector shapes. I refined the illustration for improved clarity and balance while preserving the joyful expression central to their mission. I enhanced the color palette to create a polished, vibrant look and produced full-color and monochrome variations, plus icon versions for profile photos and small-scale use.
