Social Media Designer

Created visual content supporting mental health advocacy at Un/Do Mindset, working collaboratively with Creative Directors and writers to challenge stigma and uplift communities through thoughtful, accessible design.

Client Un/Do Mindset
Timeline July 1–September 30, 2024
Role Social Media Design

The Challenge

Un/Do Mindset needed cohesive social media graphics that effectively communicated sensitive mental health topics while maintaining emotional impact, accessibility, and brand consistency. Each design had to complement written content without overshadowing the message, creating a balance between visual interest and readability.

The Solution

Working closely with Creative and Assistant Creative Directors plus the writing department, I developed visual concepts that emphasized clarity and emotional resonance. I explored typography, created mobile-optimized layouts, and maintained consistency across colors and typefaces. Through storytelling and intentional design, my work strengthened Un/Do's online presence and deepened engagement with their mental health advocacy mission.
