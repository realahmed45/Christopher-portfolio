Bishop Ranch Digital Billboard

Designed a high-resolution 22"×28" digital lobby billboard for UC Davis Bay Area MBA program featuring student Spencer Wetzel with testimonial, career statistics, and scholarship QR code.

Client UC Davis Graduate School of Management
Timeline October 11–17, 2024
Role Digital Signage Design

The Challenge

UC Davis' Graduate School of Management needed eye-catching digital signage to promote their Bay Area MBA program across multiple Bishop Ranch office buildings. The challenge was to adapt existing online ad creative into a high-resolution vertical format (22"×28") while maintaining brand consistency and maximizing visual impact in professional lobby environments.

The Solution

Working closely with Tim Akin and Lisa Nguyen, I selected the strongest photo of Spencer Wetzel from the GSM digital asset library and incorporated his testimonial in a branded blue text box. I highlighted key career success statistics and applied UC Davis brand colors and watercolor textures. Through several rounds of feedback, I refined layout, typography, and alignment, added the BAMBA 20th anniversary logo, and integrated a custom QR code linking to scholarship details.
