Bishop Ranch Digital Billboard

Digital lobby signage for UC Davis MBA at the Bishop Ranch office buildings. Adapted campaign assets to 22x28" portrait specifications with multiple layout variations.

Client UC Davis Bay Area MBA
Timeline October 11–17, 2024
Role Digital Signage

The Challenge

Create eye-catching digital signage that effectively communicates UC Davis MBA's brand while adapting existing campaign assets to the specific display requirements of Bishop Ranch's lobby screens.

The challenge was to maintain brand consistency and visual impact while working within the constraints of a 22x28" vertical portrait format, ensuring the content remained readable and engaging from various viewing distances in a professional lobby environment.

The Solution

I carefully analyzed the existing campaign materials and reimagined them for the vertical portrait format. By adjusting hierarchy, typography, and visual balance, I created multiple layout variations that maintain brand consistency while maximizing visual impact in the lobby environment.

Each variation was designed to be attention-grabbing yet professional, ensuring the UC Davis MBA brand stands out while respecting the corporate environment of the Bishop Ranch buildings.
